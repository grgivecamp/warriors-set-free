Free and Confidential

As a non-profit, Warriors Set Free provides no-cost, private counseling services to current and former military members. There are no material obstacles (and no excuses) between you and help.

Biblical and Faith-Based

Prayer is our most powerful weapon. Using teachings from the Gospel, together we'll lay a groundwork for forgiveness and healing through Christ's power.

A Network of Brothers

Peer mentors are veterans for all five branches of the military who have undertaken the same process of healing. Find a unit who understands what you went through.

You are not alone

Warriors Set Free is a ministry run by veterans, for veterans. Every volunteer has direct experience with the problems you face after returning home.
